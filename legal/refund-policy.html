Refund Policy

Last Updated: May 1, 2023

1. Introduction

At SH Infotech, we are committed to ensuring your satisfaction with our services. This Refund Policy outlines the terms and conditions for refunds related to our staffing solutions, IT services, and other offerings. By using our services, you agree to the terms of this Refund Policy.

2. Staffing Services Refund Policy

For our staffing services, the following refund terms apply:

  • Candidate Replacement Guarantee: If a candidate placed by SH Infotech leaves within 90 days of employment, we will provide a replacement candidate at no additional cost, subject to the terms of your service agreement.
  • Service Fee Refunds: If you are not satisfied with our staffing services, you may be eligible for a partial refund of service fees, depending on the stage of the recruitment process and as specified in your service agreement.
  • Cancellation Before Placement: If you cancel our staffing services before a candidate is placed, you may be entitled to a refund minus administrative costs and expenses incurred up to the point of cancellation.

3. IT Services Refund Policy

For our IT services, including software development, consulting, and technical support, the following refund terms apply:

  • Project-Based Services: For project-based services, refunds are handled according to the milestones and deliverables specified in your service agreement. If we fail to deliver as agreed, you may be entitled to a full or partial refund.
  • Subscription Services: For subscription-based services, refunds are generally not provided for the current billing period. However, you may cancel your subscription at any time to prevent future charges.
  • Consulting Services: For consulting services, refunds are evaluated on a case-by-case basis, depending on the nature of the service and the extent to which it has been delivered.

4. Refund Process

To request a refund, please follow these steps:

  1. Contact our customer service team at refunds@shinfotech.com or call +91-8977041015.
  2. Provide your name, contact information, and details about the service for which you are requesting a refund.
  3. Explain the reason for your refund request.
  4. Our team will review your request and respond within 5 business days.
  5. If your refund is approved, it will be processed within 10 business days.

5. Non-Refundable Items

The following items are generally non-refundable:

  • Services that have been fully delivered and accepted by you.
  • Custom development work that has been completed according to your specifications.
  • Administrative fees and expenses incurred in the process of providing services.
  • Services where the refund period specified in your service agreement has expired.

6. Payment of Refunds

Refunds will be issued using the same payment method used for the original purchase whenever possible. If this is not possible, we will work with you to determine an alternative refund method.

7. Disputes

If you disagree with our refund decision, you may escalate your concern to our management team by emailing management@shinfotech.com. We will review your case and provide a final decision within 15 business days.

8. Changes to This Refund Policy

We reserve the right to modify this Refund Policy at any time. Changes will be effective immediately upon posting on our website. We encourage you to review this Refund Policy periodically for any changes.
